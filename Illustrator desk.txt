==Overview==
The Illustrator desk is a [[reporting]] station which creates draft graphics. It employes a [[reporter]] employee with the [[illustrator]] skill. The base production time is based on the [[story file]]. It can be further enhanced by illustrator [[accessories]].

==Availability==
The Illustrator desk is available at the [[Photo Academy]], located in [[Brooklyn]]. It costs 16 [[Influence Point]]s to unlock, and has no purchase pre-requisits once the location has been unlocked.
